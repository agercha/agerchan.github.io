OPEN SCULPTURE

In spring 2022, I took Open Sculpture with Professor Ginger Brooks Takahashi, for which I worked on three pieces. These are listed in the opposite order they were made in.

I believe I had mixed success with these projects. In the first piece, Painted Table, I was finding my footing as it was my first experience in the formaized art space. I am happy with the idea, but I wish I had gone further and used more tools available to me in the sculpture/installation medium. I took this with me, implementing sound in my second piece, Soldat, and exploring relational aesthetics in Conversation Vareniki Lunch.
